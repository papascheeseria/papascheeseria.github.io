Establish another successful restaurant enterprise and become a master of grilled sandwiches! Papa’s Cheeseria offers you to start from the basic kitchen and turn it into an ultimate burger manufacture. This town needs some delicious toasts and you are here to prepare them!

How to Play Papa’s Cheeseria

Firstly, Papa’s Cheeseria needs you to select the main hero of the game. Pick up one of the standard workers of stylise one yourself. Remember that you create the first impression about your café, so pay attention to the style. Once the personage is crafted, you are ready to open a new chapter of tasty adventures!

The algorithm of sandwich preparation in the game is quite easy to memorise, but often you will need to complete two and more orders at the same time, so always keep in mind these steps:

  • Pick up the buns and cheese. Any recipe in Papa’s Cheeseria starts with the bread and cheese that is going to melt and create an exquisite taste of the meal. Each customer likes different variations, so be attentive when assembling their sandwiches, as the game doesn’t forgive serious mistakes!
  • Add the ingredients according to the order. In this game, ingredient placement is extremely important. Follow the recipe strictly and do exactly as your visitor wished, even if it looks absurd.
  • Grill your creation. Frying the burger is one of the most demanding moments. In Papa’s Cheeseria there are several levels of crispness that are also stated in each order. Acquire a timer for every level to fully control this process.
  • Prepare the side dish. Everybody knows that one of the best additions to a good grilled sandwich is crispy French fries! Papa’s Cheeseria game offers two variants of them and you need to be careful not to confuse them!
  • Serve your visitor. Each process will be evaluated by the client, so in Papa’s Cheeseria game it is equally important to assemble the burger neatly and to make French fries perfectly golden, enhancing them with the appropriate toppings. The last customer in the game is always the toughest judge and it means you remain focused until the very end!

The better your final result in Papa’s Cheeseria is, the more tips you are going to receive. Until the payday comes, the game allows you to use only your tips for purchasing various improvements and decorations, so make sure you distribute your finances wisely.

Design a Supreme Restaurant

Management is also one of the crucial aspects of the game. In between the sessions in Papa’s Cheeseria you may spend the salary and tips on various upgrades, from the bell that will ring when a new customer wants to make an order to colourful wallpapers. If you line up several items in one style you may even get an extra bonus.

Also, don’t forget to update your specials! In Papa’s Cheeseria you will sometimes meet people with rare orders. Complete them well and receive a unique recipe that will get you even more power-ups! Every day in the game can offer a new special, so don’t be afraid to experiment with your meal of the day.
